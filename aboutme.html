Hey there!

I'm a digital product designer based in Seattle, WA.

As a designer, my mission is to set the people I design for and work with up for success through empathy, design thinking and product strategy. I make it possible by asking the right questions, solving the right problems and facilitating close collaboration between stakeholders.

Apart from design, I enjoy reading books, cooking, listening to podcasts or playing with my dog, Omi. In a parallel universe where I'm not a designer, I'd be a chef in my own restaurant cooking and serving Indian food, the way it's supposed to be done.

If you're interested in collaborating or just want to have a fun conversation about something interesting, I'd love to connect!
